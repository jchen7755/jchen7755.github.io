Welcome to Judgy Cat and Supplies!

At Judgy Cat and Supplies serves cats and cat lovers in two ways: we provide high-quality supplies for beloved pets, and we actively work to find homes for cats in need. Our store offers everything from premium cat food to unique toys and accessories. Meanwhile, our rescue partnerships help shelter cats receive medical care, find foster homes, and ultimately connect with their perfect families. When you shop with us, you're not just caring for your cat – you're helping save lives.

Building families, one paw at a time!
